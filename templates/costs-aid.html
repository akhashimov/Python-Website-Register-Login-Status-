Tuition fee and costs

At Globus univesity, we do not charge any fee for being a student or studying at Globus University, because we want to ensure that education is available for everyone.
However,conducting examinations involves costs, for this reason we have to charge our students if they want to take examinations.


At the moment we cannot precisely answer the exact cost of examination - we are working on it.

Nevertheless, we will provide full and partial scholarships for incoming high potential students.
